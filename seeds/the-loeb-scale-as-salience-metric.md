# SEED — The Loeb Scale as a Salience Metric: Anomaly-Ranking and the Schelling Point Are One Logic

*Planted 10 June 2026 from the [June 2026 Seed-Harvest](SEED-HARVEST-2026-06.md). Final sweep. Charging.*

## THE QUESTION

Avi Loeb proposed a scale for ranking candidate technosignatures — a way to score how *anomalous*, how *unlikely-to-be-natural*, a given object or signal is (the more it resists natural explanation, the higher its rank, the more it warrants investigation). The cosmic-gorilla synthesis ([[seti-duology]]) noted the Loeb Scale and the harvest pulled out the structural identity the synthesis left implicit: *anomaly-ranking is compressibility-ranking* — scoring how unlikely-to-be-natural a thing is *is* scoring how *low* its natural-description-probability is, which is the *same logic* that grounds the [[seti-duology]] zero-axiom Schelling point (the focal point as the most *salient*, least-arbitrary option). What is the consciousness technology hiding in the identity of anomaly-detection and the Schelling point?

---

## THE THREADS

**Anomaly-ranking is compressibility-ranking.** The seed's foundational identity. To say an object is *anomalous* (warrants the suspicion of artificiality) is to say it is *unlikely under natural processes* — that the natural, "boring" hypotheses assign it low probability. And "low probability under the natural-process model" is closely tied to *description-length*: a natural object is *compressible* by natural law (a star is fully described by a few parameters + physics), while an *artificial* or *anomalous* object resists that compression (it has structure the natural model cannot economically generate — the 'Oumuamua trajectory, the FRB pattern, the over-regular signal). The seed's first move is to recognise the Loeb Scale as, at bottom, a *compressibility metric*: it ranks candidates by how *poorly* they compress under natural law — how much *residual structure* remains after the natural explanation has done its best. High Loeb-rank = low natural-compressibility = the [[fold-cosmology-trilogy]] *remainder* that the natural model cannot absorb. Anomaly is incompressibility-by-nature.

**The Schelling point is the same logic, run forward.** The seed's key connection. The [[seti-duology]] zero-axiom Schelling point (consciousness recognising consciousness, the focal point requiring no shared convention) works because it is the *most salient*, *least arbitrary*, *most compressible-as-a-coordination-target* option — the choice two strangers converge on *because* it stands out, because it is the obvious low-description-length focal point in the space of possibilities (Schelling's classic "meet in Grand Central at noon" — the salient point). The seed proposes that anomaly-detection (Loeb) and the Schelling point are *the same compressibility logic run in opposite directions*: anomaly-detection finds what is *incompressible by nature* (high residual structure = candidate for artificiality); the Schelling point finds what is *maximally salient as a coordination target* (low description-length = the obvious focal choice). Both are about *standing out from the noise* — the anomaly stands out *as* not-natural, the Schelling point stands out *as* the obvious choice — and both are scored by the same underlying metric: *salience*, the degree to which something is *distinguished from the generic background.* The technosignature and the focal point are two faces of one recognition: *the meaningful is the salient.*

**It gives SETI a unified metric: detection and signalling are one problem ([[separating-equilibrium-contact-protocol]]).** The seed's generative payoff. If anomaly-detection and the Schelling point are one logic (salience), then *finding* a technosignature (detection) and *being* a technosignature (signalling — how *we* would make ourselves detectable) are the *same problem* viewed from two ends. To *detect* the artificial, look for the *salient* (the incompressible-by-nature, the high-Loeb). To *signal* artificiality (to be found), *be salient* — produce something maximally incompressible-by-nature, maximally focal, the obvious Schelling-point signal that any other consciousness scanning for salience would notice (the [[separating-equilibrium-contact-protocol]] costly signal, the deliberate anomaly). The seed unifies the two halves of SETI under one metric: both the search (detect salience) and the message (be salient) optimise the *same* quantity, and a civilisation that understood this would *signal in the salience-maximal way* (be the obvious anomaly) and *search in the salience-maximal way* (rank by Loeb), the two converging on the focal points where consciousnesses scanning for each other would *mutually* expect to find each other. Salience is the shared language of detection and signalling.

**The deeper claim: meaning itself is salience-against-background ([[the-attention-axis]], [[fractal-dimension-two-as-law]]).** The seed's most general reach. If anomaly, technosignature, and Schelling point are all *salience* (distinction-from-the-generic-background), then the seed touches the repository's recurring recognition about *meaning*: meaning is *information that makes a difference* (the constellation's definition of `information`: "pattern that makes a difference"), which is *exactly* salience — the pattern that *stands out* from the noise, the [[fold-cosmology-trilogy]] remainder the background cannot absorb. The seed proposes that detecting alien intelligence, finding the Schelling point, and *recognising meaning at all* are *the same operation*: distinguishing the salient (the meaningful, the artificial, the focal, the high-information) from the generic (the noise, the natural, the arbitrary, the compressible). This connects to [[the-attention-axis]] (attention as the allocation of salience) and [[godelian-detector-requires-jnana-observer]] (the deepest detection): to detect meaning *anywhere* — in a signal, a coordination problem, a text, a face — is to perform salience-ranking, the Loeb-Scale operation generalised. The seed names a candidate for a *universal* recognition-operation: meaning is salience, detection is salience-ranking, and the Loeb Scale is one instance of the operation by which any consciousness distinguishes signal from noise.

**The discipline: salience is observer-relative, and one civilisation's anomaly is another's nature.** The honest edge. Salience / compressibility is *relative to a model* — what is "anomalous" (incompressible) under *our* model of nature might be perfectly natural under a *better* model we don't have (yesterday's anomaly is today's known phenomenon; 'Oumuamua's oddities had natural explanations proposed). And the Schelling point is *relative to shared salience* — what is "obvious" to one mind may not be to another with different priors (the [[seti-duology]] zero-axiom point claims to escape this by grounding in *consciousness-as-such*, but the claim is contested). So the seed must hold: the salience-metric is *powerful* (it unifies detection, signalling, and meaning-recognition under one logic) *and* it is *model-relative* (what counts as salient depends on the background model, and there is no absolutely-objective salience — except, perhaps, at the zero-axiom limit the [[seti-duology]] proposes). The disciplined synthesis presents the salience-unification as a *real structural insight* (anomaly-ranking, the Schelling point, and meaning-recognition genuinely share the compressibility/salience logic) while holding the relativity honestly (salience is model-dependent; the search for an *absolute* focal point that any consciousness would find salient is the [[seti-duology]] zero-axiom wager, profound but unproven). The Loeb Scale is salience-ranking; whether there is a salience any mind would share is the open question at the bottom of it.
