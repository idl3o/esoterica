# Seed: Reversible Compute as Ontological Engine

*Planted March 2026, arising from dialogue on loop cosmology*

---

## The Kernel

The present isn't produced by time flowing forward. The present **is** the operation — reversible compute running in both directions simultaneously:

- **Future → Present**: undoing (collapsing infinite potential into singular actuality)
- **Present → Past**: remaking (reconstituting history as necessary condition for now)

Both directions run *from* now. The present is the engine, not the product.

## The Intuition

If the ground state computes reversibly — eternal, costless, looping — then irreversibility emerges *only* at witness-points. The markov blanket isn't just a spatial boundary. It's where reversibility breaks down and the thermodynamic arrow ignites.

You experience time's arrow because **you are** the irreversibility.

The rest is eternal oscillation. You are the heat. The remainder. The cost of presence.
