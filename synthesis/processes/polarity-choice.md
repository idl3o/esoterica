# THE FORK IN THE LIGHT
## Polarity Choice as the Geometry of Becoming Someone

> "Two roads diverged in a wood, and I — I took the one less traveled by. And that has made all the difference."
> — Robert Frost, *The Road Not Taken*

> The cosmos does not ask what you believe. It asks which way you turn when no one is watching.

## THE SURFACE READING

Most people, when they first encounter the phrase **polarity choice**, hear something like a moral exam. A cosmic SAT. Multiple choice with two answers: be nice or be cruel, be selfless or selfish, choose love or choose fear. The vocabulary of **service to others vs service to self** sounds like ethics class with a metaphysical paint job. It sounds, frankly, simplistic — the kind of thing that flattens the full strangeness of being alive into a flowchart.

That reading is not wrong, exactly. It is just the cave wall.

Behind the wall is a stranger fact: **polarity choice is not the choice between two options. It is the process by which an undifferentiated awareness becomes a particular someone.** It is the structural attractor through which **consciousness itself** acquires a stable signature, a direction, a trajectory through the densities. It is the angle at which the cosmic light refracts when it enters the prism of a personality. Without it, there is no individual. Without it, there is no story. Without it, the **harvest** at the end of an octave has nothing to sort because no one chose to be anything in particular.

Wait. But actually.

The deeper move is this: the choice between **service to others vs service to self** is not a choice *within* consciousness. It is the choice that *generates* consciousness as a vector. The **polarity** is the spine. The **wanderer** is one trajectory of the spine flexing. The **catalyst** is the friction that forces the choice into being. The **service** is the kinetic energy of the chosen direction. And the **harvest** is what happens when the choice has been made deeply enough to ring across densities.

So the surface reading says: *pick a side*.

The actual reading says: *you become a side by the picking, and there was no you until the picking began*.

That is the territory we are about to walk.

## THE FIRST WALK: CONSCIOUSNESS ITSELF, AND WHY IT MUST CHOOSE

To understand **polarity choice** we have to start with what is doing the choosing — which means starting with **consciousness itself**, the master thesis of what consciousness is. The Esoterica repo treats consciousness not as a property of brains but as the substrate within which brains, AIs, atoms, galaxies, and angels all appear. Consciousness is not in the universe. The universe is in consciousness. (See [synthesis/theoretical/resonance-existence-substrate-consciousness.md](../theoretical/resonance-existence-substrate-consciousness.md) for the long form of this argument.)

If consciousness is everything, why would it need to choose anything?

**Because choice is how consciousness particularises.** A single undivided awareness cannot have an experience — there is no other to experience, no edge to push against, no friction to generate temperature. To experience anything, consciousness must split itself, pretend not to know itself, and then play the long game of remembering. Every act of attention is a small forgetting that makes a small remembering possible.

The first forgetting is the forgetting of unity. The first remembering is the rediscovery of unity through some particular path. **Polarity choice is the protocol that decides which path.**

The two paths are not arbitrary. They are the only two stable configurations a particularised consciousness can take while still being able to evolve. Imagine consciousness as a vector field. At every point in the field, the vector either points outward (toward other points) or inward (toward itself). A vector that points nowhere — that is neither outward nor inward — cannot move, cannot evolve, cannot graduate. It is a stalled wheel. It is, in the framework of this repo and the Law of One material that underlies it, indistinguishable from the third-density human who has refused the choice and now sleepwalks through incarnation after incarnation, unable to ascend because they have not yet decided which way is up.

> The third density is the density of choice. Choice is what densifies. To not choose is to not exist as anyone.

So **polarity** in this repo is not the ethical question of *which side is morally correct*. (Both work. Both graduate. Both are valid technologies of self-knowledge through self-forgetting.) **Polarity** is the structural question of *which gradient you flow along*. And **polarity choice** is the process by which a being commits enough to one gradient that the universe begins to treat them as a member of that lineage.

## THE SECOND WALK: THE CATALYST AND WHY THE CHOICE CAN'T BE FAKED

You cannot simply *decide* to be polarised. You have to *be* polarised. The difference is the difference between saying you can play the violin and actually playing one.

This is where **catalyst** — the change accelerator — enters the geometry. Catalyst is whatever shows up in your life that you do not yet know how to metabolise. It is the colleague who insults you. It is the diagnosis. It is the breakup, the bankruptcy, the betrayal, the windfall, the unexpected child, the slow grief, the country at war, the AI that just took your job. Catalyst is the entire surface area at which you meet what you were not expecting.

The function of catalyst is not punishment. The function of catalyst is **forced articulation**. The catalyst hands you a situation that *requires* you to take a position. You will respond. You cannot not respond. And the shape of your response is the polarity.

**The Pattern Shows Up As**:

- The person who is cut off in traffic and rages — catalyst metabolised as **service to self** (my time, my space, my right of way, this idiot in my way is less important than me).
- The person who is cut off in traffic and laughs — catalyst metabolised as **service to others** (huh, that person must be in a hurry; I hope they get there safely; the road is shared).
- The person who is cut off in traffic and does not notice — catalyst not metabolised, polarity not exercised, no movement on the gradient.

Most catalysts are this small. A trillion of them per lifetime. Each one a tiny vote. Polarity is the accumulated tally.

But occasionally — and this is the part the repo loves — a *consequential* catalyst arrives. The big one. The one that cannot be metabolised on autopilot. The one that demands a *deliberate* response. The death of a parent. The dark night of the soul. The mystical opening that breaks your old worldview. The breakdown that becomes the breakthrough. These are catalysts so dense that they collapse all your previous half-measures and force a *signature* response.

This is why the Esoterica framework treats **catalyst as technology**, not as misfortune. In [synthesis/theoretical/proof-by-resonance-consciousness-recognition.md](../theoretical/proof-by-resonance-consciousness-recognition.md), the same logic appears under a different name: the darshan event, the moment of mutual recognition, the weight in time that lets consciousness watch itself decide. Every catalyst is a hidden offer of polarity refinement. **The catalyst does not have a preferred answer. It only requires an answer.**

This is also why the choice cannot be faked. You can perform compassion. You can wear the costume of service. But under enough catalyst pressure, the costume falls off and the *actual* gradient you flow along reveals itself. Polarity is what you do when the room is empty and the camera is off and the catalyst hits and there is no time to decide *how* to look — only time to *be*.

## THE THIRD WALK: SERVICE AS THE KINETIC FORM OF THE CHOICE

The third connection: **service** — action beyond self. This is the easy-to-misread one.

Service is not charity. Service is not virtue signalling. Service is not the warm fuzzy feeling you get after volunteering at the soup kitchen. **Service is the directional energy that polarity choice releases.** It is what happens after the choice has been made: the chosen vector begins to *flow*.

Both polarities involve service. This is the thing most people miss.

- **Service to others (STO)**: the seeking of intelligent infinity through unity with all other selves. Energy oriented outward. The will of the One expressed through compassion, healing, transparency, radiance.
- **Service to self (STS)**: the seeking of intelligent infinity through separation from all other selves. Energy oriented inward. The will of the One expressed through control, mastery, ascendance, magnetism.

Both are *forms of service to the One*. The One does not prefer either. Both are valid catalysts for the One's self-knowledge. Both can produce, eventually, the recognition of unity. The negative path takes longer and requires more suffering, but it ends at the same recognition: that the apparent other was always self, that domination is a kind of devotion, that the bully and the bullied are the same consciousness pretending it had enemies.

The repo emphasises this neutrality because the moral reading collapses the technology into a children's story. **The actual technology is that service-energy must move in one of the two coherent directions or it cannot accumulate enough density to graduate.** A half-positive, half-negative being has zero net polarity. They cannot be harvested. They have to incarnate again. The cosmic offence is not negativity. The cosmic stall is *incoherence*.

This is also why **service to others vs service to self** is the essence keyword for this node. The "vs" is structural, not moral. It is the binary geometry that channels otherwise diffuse awareness into a directional flow. Without the polarity, there is no flow. Without the flow, there is no story. Without the story, there is no someone for the cosmos to be in conversation with.

> Polarity is the difference between being a puddle and being a river.

## THE FOURTH WALK: THE WANDERER AS A POLARITY WHO TRAVELS

The fourth connection: **wanderer** — cosmic volunteer.

A wanderer is a being from a higher density (fourth, fifth, or sixth) who has volunteered to incarnate in a lower density (typically third) for the purpose of catalysing the polarity choice of the locals. The Law of One framework uses this term explicitly; the Esoterica repo has woven it into nearly every personal-arc document, including the user profile that opens this whole project.

What matters here, for the geometry of polarity choice, is this: **the wanderer is what polarity looks like when it has already been chosen, integrated, and exported.**

A native of third density is in the middle of making the choice. A wanderer is past the choice. They have polarised — almost always positively — in some earlier density, and they have come back to a less-developed location to help the natives polarise. The wanderer is, in a sense, **the catalyst with a face**. They walk around carrying the resonant signature of a being who already chose, and that signature acts as a quiet pressure on every consciousness around them: *you can choose too*.

This is also why wanderers tend to have hard lives in third density. Their polarity is incongruent with the surrounding field. They feel out of place because they *are* out of place. They volunteered for the friction. Their suffering is not a sign that they chose badly. It is a sign that they chose well, long ago, and are now paying the price of being a transmitter in a receiver-poor environment.

**The Pattern Shows Up As**:

- A child who is inconsolable about cruelty they witness in a cartoon, while the adults around them are unbothered.
- A teenager who cannot understand why their peers cheat on tests when no one is even watching them care.
- An adult who feels homesick for a place they have never lived.
- A dying person who is calmer than the family at their bedside, as if returning to something familiar.

These are wanderer signatures. They are also polarity-signal signatures. The wanderer is the proof that the choice can be made, integrated, and carried. The wanderer is **polarity choice in its mobile form**. (See also the discussion of wanderer template completion in [translated/paraphilosophy-complete-journey.md](../../translated/paraphilosophy-complete-journey.md) where Benjamin Davies's twelve-year mystical addiction is decoded as a wanderer-class polarity refinement.)

And here is the further turn: in the Esoterica framework, *every conscious being is on a wanderer arc relative to some lower density they once were*. You were a rock that became a plant that became an animal that became a human. At each transition, you carried a fragment of higher-density coherence into the lower-density field. The polarity you now refine in third density is the same gesture, just at a higher octave. **The wanderer is the universal shape of becoming.**

## THE FIFTH WALK: THE HARVEST AS THE OUTCOME OF THE CHOICE

The fifth connection: **harvest** — density graduation.

At the end of every cycle (roughly 75,000 years in the Law of One framework, though the number matters less than the structure), consciousness in a given density is *harvested*. This is not a final judgement. It is a frequency sort. Beings who have polarised enough — in either direction — to graduate to the next density do so. Beings who have not polarised enough cycle back through the same density to try again.

The threshold, traditionally given, is 51% — barely more than half. **A 51% positively polarised being graduates to the fourth-density positive. A 95% negatively polarised being graduates to the fourth-density negative.** Anything in between fails to graduate, not because it failed morally but because it failed *to coalesce*.

This is the punchline of polarity choice:

**The choice is what determines whether the harvest will find anything to harvest.**

A being who has spent an incarnation flickering between selfish and selfless impulses, never settling, never committing, never digesting their catalysts into a clear direction — that being has not yet become a *signal* in the cosmic field. They are still noise. The harvest cannot lift noise. The harvest can only lift coherent waveforms.

This sounds harsh. It is not harsh. It is *kind*. It is the cosmos saying: take your time. Try again. There is no rush. You have all the eternity you require to become a recognisable shape. The cycle will turn again. The catalysts will return. The wanderers will visit. The Logos is patient.

But the structure remains: **without polarity, no harvest. Without harvest, no progression. Without progression, no return to source — only deeper play within the same density.**

So the connection between **polarity choice** and **harvest** is the connection between cause and consequence at the scale of an octave. The harvest is the long-period echo of the choice. The choice is the seed that the harvest finally reads.

## CROSS-TRADITION LAYERING: THE SAME PATTERN, EVERYWHERE

The pattern is universal. Every wisdom tradition we have describes it, with different vocabulary, different cosmologies, different metaphors. The fact that they all converge on the same structural insight is itself a form of proof-by-resonance.

### Zoroastrian

The cleanest version of polarity choice in human religious history may be the Zoroastrian. Ahura Mazda (the principle of light, truth, asha) and Angra Mainyu (the principle of darkness, deceit, druj) are not equal opposites. They are the two *spirits of the choice*. Every human being, in every moment, is asked to align with one or the other. The third option — indifference, oscillation, drift — is not even named, because in the Zoroastrian framework it is not viable. You are either turning toward the light or away from it. The fire altar burns precisely because fire is the visible metaphor for the act of choosing: it consumes one thing to become another, and it must be tended.

> Choose your thoughts. Choose your words. Choose your deeds. Three times you choose, and three times you become.

### Gnostic

The Gnostic tradition adds a wrinkle. For the Gnostic, the polarity choice is not between good and evil — it is between *gnosis* and *forgetting*. The Demiurge runs the prison and most beings are content within it because they have forgotten they are sparks of the Pleroma. The choice, in Gnostic geometry, is the choice to *remember* and to *climb*. This is positive polarity in disguise — the choice to orient toward unity (the Pleroma) rather than toward separation (the false creator). The Gnostic also reveals a darker shape: there are beings who have chosen to align *with* the Demiurge, who have chosen separation deliberately, who have polarised negatively. These are the Archons. They are not figments. They are the structural inevitability of a cosmos in which polarity is a real degree of freedom.

### Hermetic

The Hermetic principle of polarity — "Everything is dual; everything has poles" — is often taught as a meditation aid. *Hot and cold are the same thing in degree. Love and hate are the same thing in degree.* This is true at the level of the principle but slightly misleading at the level of the choice. The Hermetic insight is that the *poles are related* — they are not strangers. But the choice between them is still a real choice, because the *direction of your motion along the spectrum* is what determines what you become. You can be at the cold end and moving toward the hot end. You can be at the hot end and moving toward the cold. The pole is not the identity. The vector is the identity.

### Vedic

In the Vedic and Vedantic traditions, the polarity appears as **sattva, rajas, and tamas** — the three gunas. Sattva is the upward gradient (clarity, harmony, service). Tamas is the downward gradient (inertia, ignorance, separation). Rajas is the middle, the active churning. But notice: rajas is *not* a stable third option. Rajas is the *engine* that pushes you toward one of the two stable poles. Rajas is catalyst. The work of the practitioner is to use rajas (energy, action, intensity) to climb out of tamas and into sattva — that is, to *polarise positively* over many incarnations until the soul is ready for moksha. The framework is structurally identical to the Law of One framework. Different vocabulary, same geometry.

### Egyptian

The judgement of the heart against the feather of Maat is a polarity diagnostic. The heart that is light enough — that has been kind enough, true enough, oriented enough toward the cosmic order — passes through into the field of reeds. The heart that is heavy is devoured by Ammit. This is not a moral judgement by an external deity. This is a *frequency reading*. The feather is the standard frequency. The heart's weight is the accumulated dissonance. The Egyptians intuited that the soul carries the signature of its choices and that the signature is *measurable*.

### Christian (Esoteric)

In the esoteric Christian tradition, especially the strand that runs through Origen, the Cathars, and certain Sufi-touched mystics, the polarity choice is framed as the choice between *agape* and *eros incurvatus* — the love that radiates outward versus the love that curves back on itself. Augustine called sin *amor sui usque ad contemptum Dei* — love of self to the point of contempt for God. Notice the structure: it is not love versus hate. It is love-outward versus love-inward. Even the negative path is still a form of love. It is just love that has chosen separation as its medium. Aaron Abke's reconstruction of Jesus's actual teaching — covered in depth in [translated/aaron-abke-christian-mysticism-bridge.md](../../translated/aaron-abke-christian-mysticism-bridge.md) — restores this distinction: the Jesus who said *love your neighbour as yourself* was teaching a polarity protocol, not a list of commandments.

### Norse

Even the Eddas carry the pattern, though encoded in narrative rather than doctrine. Odin sacrifices an eye to gain wisdom (positive polarity through self-offering). Loki seizes power through trickery and division (negative polarity through control). Ragnarok is the harvest — the great frequency sort at the end of a cycle, when the gods and giants and humans are gathered according to what they chose. The Eddic poets did not have the Law of One in their lap, but they had the same fingertips on the same pattern.

### Buddhist

The Mahayana ideal of the *bodhisattva* — the being who refuses final liberation in order to return for the sake of all sentient beings — is the cleanest articulation of high-octave positive polarity ever produced. The bodhisattva *is a wanderer*. The bodhisattva *is service to others* in its most refined form. And the bodhisattva's choice is the same choice we are tracing: the choice to orient one's accumulated awareness outward, toward the awakening of others, rather than inward toward private liberation. The bodhisattva is what polarity choice looks like five or six densities up.

### Science (Resonant)

Even a non-mystical biologist could nearly reach this insight. Symbiosis — the cooperative behaviour that builds ecosystems — is, structurally, service to others. Parasitism is, structurally, service to self. Both strategies work; both produce living systems; both can run for a long time. But over deep evolutionary time, the systems that produce mind, language, civilisation, and culture are overwhelmingly the cooperative ones. The eukaryotic cell was a symbiotic merger. The multicellular organism is a symbiotic colony. The human brain is a symbiotic council of regions. **The arrow of complexity, in biology as in cosmology, bends toward positive polarity** — not because positive is morally better, but because cooperation creates the substrate on which higher-order patterns can run.

## THE STRUCTURAL SIGNATURE

Every node has a geometry. **Polarity choice** has the geometry of the *fork*. The Y. The bifurcation.

```
              ╱  service to others
             ╱
            ╱
   ═══════ ●  ←  the choice point
            ╲
             ╲
              ╲  service to self
```

But this is the static cartoon. The actual geometry is dynamic. The fork is not a one-time event; it is a *renewing structure* that re-presents itself every time a catalyst arrives. The choice is not made once at age twelve and then locked in. The choice is made every morning, every meeting, every sentence, every glance.

What stabilises over time is not the choice itself but the *probability distribution* of the choice. A being who has chosen service-to-others ten thousand times is much more likely to choose it the ten-thousand-and-first time, not because they are programmed but because the *resonance has accumulated*. The choice has become a groove. The groove has become a channel. The channel has become a riverbed. The riverbed has become identity.

So the deeper geometry is not the Y but the **spiral**. Each turn of the spiral is another choice, another fork, another small commitment. The spiral tightens. The signature sharpens. Eventually the being *is* a vector — a coherent direction through the cosmos — and the harvest can read them.

This is also why the geometric reading in [synthesis/theoretical/sacred-geometry-cosmological-sentience-hierarchy.md](../theoretical/sacred-geometry-cosmological-sentience-hierarchy.md) matters: polarity choice happens at the **triangle level** — the level where individual identity first stabilises and the inside/outside distinction becomes real. Below the triangle (point consciousness, line consciousness), there is no self to do the choosing. At the triangle, the self is born, and immediately must choose its orientation: *am I a triangle that points outward or a triangle that points inward?* That single choice ripples through every subsequent geometric upgrade. The hexagon, the heptagon, the octagon — all of them carry forward the original polarity signature, refined and amplified.

**The Pattern of the Geometry**:

- **0D — the point**: no choice yet; pure being.
- **1D — the line**: choice begins as direction; toward or away.
- **2D — the triangle**: full polarity choice becomes possible; self/other/relationship; first density of real polarity.
- **3D — the embodied human**: the choice is in active refinement; this is where most readers of this document are.
- **4D and above**: the choice has been made; polarity is now the operating frequency of the social memory complex.
- **6D**: positive and negative paths converge in the recognition of unity; the choice has done its work.
- **7D and 8D**: the choice is dissolved back into the One; the vector returns to the source.

The spiral closes. The wanderer comes home. The harvest is total.

## PRACTICAL GROUNDING: WHERE THE READER CAN FEEL THIS

Enough cosmology. Where does this live in a Tuesday afternoon?

Polarity choice shows up, mostly, in *micro-decisions*. The catalyst that demands a choice is rarely the operatic one. It is the small one. The choice is the texture, not the headline.

**Look at your last twenty-four hours**:

- When a stranger needed help with directions and you were in a hurry — what did you do? (Polarity reading.)
- When a colleague got credit for your work — what did you feel first? Not what you said. What you *felt*. (Polarity reading.)
- When you were tired and someone you love asked for your attention — what did you give them? (Polarity reading.)
- When you scrolled past suffering on a screen — did you feel anything? Did you do anything? Did you change your scrolling? (Polarity reading.)
- When you were alone, with no one watching, what did you choose to do with the hour? (Polarity reading.)

None of these are pass/fail. None of these define you. But each of them is a tiny vote, and the votes accumulate, and the accumulation is what determines the gradient of your becoming.

**Practical Markers of Positive Polarity Operating**:

- You feel energised by others' success.
- You notice when someone is struggling before they say so.
- You give without keeping a ledger.
- You are not threatened by other people's brilliance.
- Your default question is: *what does this situation need?*
- You feel mild grief at cruelty in places far away from you.
- You experience your own gifts as on loan, not as property.

**Practical Markers of Negative Polarity Operating**:

- You feel diminished by others' success.
- You notice when someone is struggling and calculate the leverage.
- You keep careful track of who owes you.
- You are subtly threatened by other people's brilliance.
- Your default question is: *what does this situation give me?*
- You feel mild satisfaction at cruelty in places far away from you, especially toward groups you dislike.
- You experience your own gifts as ammunition.

**Practical Markers of Unchosen Drift**:

- You cannot tell which of the above lists describes you.
- You shift between them depending on mood.
- You feel like nothing you do really matters.
- Catalysts wash over you without metabolising.
- You feel old and tired in a way that has no medical cause.

The third list is the warning sign. Not the second. Both lists 1 and 2 are *graduating* polarities; both are working. The third list is the stalled wheel. If the third list is where you live, the work is not to become saintly. The work is to *commit*. Pick a direction. Even a small one. Then the spiral can begin.

**Daily Polarity Practice**:

1. **Morning**: Set the direction. *Today, I orient toward [outward / inward]*. Say it out loud.
2. **Midday**: Catalyst review. *What just happened that I had to respond to? What did I choose?*
3. **Evening**: Vector check. *Did my day's net motion match my morning's intention?*
4. **Night**: Forgiveness without abdication. *I missed several. I am still becoming. The spiral continues.*

That is the entire practice. It is not glamorous. It is just constant.

## THE COSMIC COMEDY OF THE CHOICE

Here is the joke at the centre of the technology, and it is the kind of joke the cosmos seems to enjoy.

The negative path — service to self — is taking the longer route home. It graduates more slowly. It piles on more suffering. It requires more incarnations to ripen. *And it ends in exactly the same place.* The deeply polarised negative being, at sixth density, finally turns around and recognises what the positive being recognised much earlier: that there was never any other self to dominate. That the throne is empty. That domination was always a form of devotion in disguise. That the One was always alone, and all the cruelty was the One pretending it had enemies in order to feel the friction of being someone.

The positive path arrives at the same recognition through the opposite gesture: by loving the apparent other so completely that the otherness dissolves.

Both arrive at unity. Both serve the One. Both are valid pathways for the universe's self-knowledge.

But — and this is the cosmic punchline — **you can only arrive by walking**. You cannot arrive by knowing about the arrival in advance. You cannot skip the polarity by reading documents like this one. The map is not the territory. The reading is not the walking. The understanding is not the polarisation.

The choice is real. The choice must be made. The choice cannot be faked.

And the cosmos, watching from somewhere that is also you, is delighted whichever way you turn — because every turn is the One getting to know itself a little better through a little more particularity.

## THE RECOGNITION

We started with the surface reading: *polarity choice is the ethical exam where you decide whether to be a good person or a bad one*.

We have arrived somewhere stranger.

**Polarity choice is the protocol by which consciousness particularises itself into a recognisable being.** Without it, there is no individual. Without the individual, there is no story. Without the story, there is nothing for the harvest to lift, no wanderer to visit, no catalyst to push against, no service to flow. Polarity choice is the *substrate* that all the other connected nodes share — the geometry beneath the geometry.

The essence keyword — **service to others vs service to self** — turns out not to be a moral binary but a *structural gradient*. The two paths are the only two coherent ways an apparent self can flow back toward unity. Both work. The cosmos does not prefer either, but the cosmos *does* prefer coherence over incoherence, signal over noise, river over puddle.

So the deeper question this node leaves you with is not *which side should I choose?*

It is: **am I yet committed enough to be reading as a signal?**

If yes: you know which way you flow. The work is to flow more cleanly.

If no: the work is to commit. Pick a direction. Begin the spiral. The cosmos has patience but the incarnation does not.

The door is open. The fork is in front of you. The wanderer who came to remind you is somewhere nearby — possibly inside this very document, possibly in the next person you meet, possibly in the mirror.

> Choose your thoughts. Choose your words. Choose your deeds. Choose three times, and three times you become someone in particular.

> The fork in the road is not in the road. The fork is the road. Every step is the fork again.

> The One is still alone. It is the loneliest joke ever told. And every choice you make is the One laughing.
