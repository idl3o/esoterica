## THE THREE WORDS: ALLOW, ACCEPT, EMBRACE

### Transcending Duality

Richard's technology for moving through the three levels:

**ALLOW** (Shadow Level):
> "When you feel a state of misery or collapse or contraction or fear or anger—anything that causes you to contract—you have to first allow it. Allow is very generous. You don't have to like it. You can hate it. Everything is encompassed in allowing. It's just allowing the feeling."

**ACCEPT** (Gift Level):
> "That allowing deepens over time. It starts to crack open the seed of that suffering. Then that allowing starts to become acceptance. Acceptance can't be done suddenly—it has to pass through the phase of allowing."

**EMBRACE** (Siddhi Level):
> "Embrace is when the shadow has been so deeply accepted that there's no part of you that wants anything to be any different from the way it is. That's utter embrace. That's a magical moment—no discipline or technique can get you there."
