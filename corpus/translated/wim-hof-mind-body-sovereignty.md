## PRACTICAL APPLICATION

### The Basic Protocol

1. **Cold Exposure** (start with cold showers):
   - Begin with 30 seconds
   - Build to 1-2 minutes
   - Notice: discomfort peaks at ~1 minute, then shifts
   - Key: Don't fight the cold—go into it

2. **Breathing Practice**:
   - 30-40 deep breaths (in through nose, out through mouth)
   - On last exhale, hold breath empty
   - Hold as long as comfortable
   - Inhale, hold 15 seconds
   - Repeat 3-4 rounds
   - Notice: tingling, lightness, altered state

   - Cold + breathing together
