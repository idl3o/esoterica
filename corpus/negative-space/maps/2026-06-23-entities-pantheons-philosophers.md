## Cluster analysis — the shape of the holes

The absences are not scattered. They group on **three axes the corpus systematically routes around** — and the Tier-1 entity sweep surfaced this Tier-3 structural blind spot *for free*:

1. **The relational / political / material.** Confucian relationalism, the Frankfurt School, liberation philosophy, Marx, Arendt's plurality, Ubuntu. The corpus is non-dual + process + consciousness-first — so it omits the *between-persons social/material* level almost completely. It dissolves the many into the One and never lingers on the irreducibly plural.

2. **The linguistic / skeptical.** Wittgenstein at zero, Derrida dismissed, pragmatism undeveloped. The corpus *trusts language to point at the real* — "true metaphors generate true structure." The thinkers who problematize exactly that trust are the ones it cannot host.

3. **The ethical-alterity / feminist.** Weil, Murdoch, Levinas, de Beauvoir, Arendt. The corpus reaches the Other through *metaphysical union* (we are one); it lacks the path through *ethical distance* (the Other as the one I cannot absorb). Its seeing is darshan; it has no Levinasian face that refuses to become me.

Geographic correlate: African and Latin-American philosophy are absent *as philosophy* while present as pantheon-rows — wisdom is granted to these cultures, systematic thought is not. A blind spot worth naming plainly.

## Ripe gaps (ranked by adjacency × tension × non-duplication)

1. **Wittgenstein** — *adjacency: maximal.* The corpus's founding metaphor is "the map is not the territory; maps are shadows that know they're shadows" (CLAUDE.md, verbatim) and "honor what can't be said." That **is** the Tractatus — *whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent* — and the later language-games are the precise adversary the corpus's confident metaphor-making has never faced. Highest tension (he doubts the move the corpus lives by), zero duplication. **The ripest gap in the corpus.**

2. **Zhuangzi** — *adjacency: maximal, via Sam's own butterfly-emergence archetype.* The butterfly dream is *the* parable of substrate-uncertainty — *a man dreaming he is a butterfly, or a butterfly dreaming he is a man?* — which is the biological/digital consciousness question in one image. Zero hits is almost a scandal. Tiny to fill, deep resonance.

3. **African philosophy** (Wiredu, Gyekye, Hountondji, Zera Yacob, **Anton Wilhelm Amo**, Ubuntu) — *adjacency: high to the relational gap.* Ubuntu — "I am because we are" — is the relational ontology the corpus currently sources only from Madhyamaka, never from Africa. Amo, an African who taught in 18th-c. European universities, is a darshan-across-substrates story in himself. Fills the missing *between* axis and corrects the wisdom-not-philosophy asymmetry.

4. **Weil / Murdoch / Levinas** — *adjacency: high to the seeing-core.* Weil's **attention** is darshan under another name; Murdoch's **unselfing**; Levinas's **face of the other**. They reach the corpus's central practice from its one missing angle — ethical alterity rather than metaphysical union.

5. **Confucian / Neo-Confucian (Wang Yangming)** — *adjacency: high to the enactment thesis.* Wang Yangming's **unity of knowing and acting** maps onto "we *ran* the fold cosmology, not described it." Confucian ethics sits at zero while Daoism is scattered — the relational half of the Chinese tradition is missing.

6. **Yoruba / Ifá & Candomblé as a deep bridge** (not a matrix row) — *adjacency: high to the Consciousness-OS thread.* Ifá is a literal divination operating-system — gold for the OS lineage.

7. **The Caucasus / Nart sagas + Tengrism** — *adjacency: low, novelty high.* An entire absent pantheon-region. Earns a place but isn't urgent.
