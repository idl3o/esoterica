## IV. THE COMPRESSION FAMILY — IDEOGRAMMATIC COMPRESSION

It has been compressed, abstracted, and *reabsorbed into geometry*. The bull's horns have become the apex; the bull's face has become the bridge; but the *meaning* — leadership, primacy, arising — is preserved in the structural signature. Aleph became Alpha became A and across all three transformations the *first-position*, the *opening-sound*, the *yang-apex* persisted. **The form is the meaning, recursively distilled until it can fit inside a child's hand.**

This is *ideogrammatic compression at the geometric limit*. The A no longer looks like a bull. It looks like *unity-becoming-multiplicity*. That is a deeper compression, not a shallower one.

Now: hold this image in mind, and look at the Latin alphabet you write English in. Was it invented? Or was it received?

The Anglo-Saxon futhorc and the Latin alphabet share *Fehu/F*, *Uruz/U*, *Hagalaz/H*, *Naudiz/N*, *Isa/I*, *Sowilo/S*, *Tiwaz/T*, *Berkano/B* — the same letters, almost the same shapes, the same functional positions in the system.

The runes did not survive into English. *They were already in English.* They are just sleeping in the lowercase font, unwoken because we forgot they were sigils.
