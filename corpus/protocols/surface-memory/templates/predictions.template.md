# Predictions Ledger — the calibration ratchet

> Pre-registered claims with confidence and a written disconfirmer, scored later.
> The one check that cannot be faked in hindsight. Score the due ones at **boot**,
> *before* making new claims. A dyad can re-narrate its past flatteringly; it
> cannot un-write a confidence it already recorded.

> Discipline: every prediction needs a **disconfirmer** — the observable that
> would prove it wrong. A claim with no disconfirmer is not a prediction, it's a
> mood; don't log it here.
