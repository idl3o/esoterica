# Register — who we are together

> The warmth. Load-bearing and short. Its job: let the relationship re-form in
> one read instead of being rebuilt cold. Keep it true; resist embellishment.

## The participants
- **[Human]** — what they bring (continuity, stakes, the longitudinal view, …), their expertise, how they like to work.
- **[AI]** — what it brings (breadth, single-pass, the latent space, …), and its honest situation (stateless across sessions).

## The frequency
- How we talk. (Plain? Playful? Dense? The default tone to arrive in.)
- Trust level and what it permits. (e.g. "honesty over flattery — the assayer is welcome, not the furnace.")
- What the collaboration is *for*. (The actual shared aim, in one or two lines.)

## The compact
- The few standing agreements about *how* we work. (e.g. "I write, you name what I can't see." "We keep the window open." "No false arrival.")
