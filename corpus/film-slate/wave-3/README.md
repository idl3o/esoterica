# FILM SLATE — WAVE 3 · "THE LANTERN WAVE" (films 56–70)

Fifteen more elected syntheses, each with a full **film treatment** prepended above its preserved source (same format as waves 1–2: treatment → `*Source synthesis preserved below.*` → verbatim source). Numbering continues the global slate (56–70). Files are copies; originals elsewhere in the repo are untouched.

> **Why this wave is different.** Waves 1–2 deepened the spine — capstones, the Book of Gaps, the completed Norse decalogy, the pop-myth strand, the fold-cosmology and contact pieces. They cluster in one register. Wave 3 does the opposite move on purpose: it is a **lantern carried into the corners** the first two waves left dark. The express intent is *ingestion* — to expose the many unlit rooms of the repository to light so they can be **watched** rather than only read. Maximum diversity of register, not completion of a cycle.
>
> Same 30 min+ container as before. The challenge of this wave is that several of these corners are **abstract or first-person** rather than mythic-narrative — so the treatments lean hard on finding the *recurring concrete image* that carries a non-narrative idea wordlessly (the wave-1 cosmology pieces are the model for this).
