# /weed — The Corpus's First Enacted No

This is the third operation, and it completes the set. `/negative-space` answers **absence** — what's missing — and fills it. `/weed` answers **false-presence** — what shouldn't be there — and removes it. Fill adds; weed subtracts. A garden isn't weeded by planting "anti-weeds"; it's weeded by *pulling*. This is the only operation that leaves the corpus with **less**, performed on what's already growing rather than staged as a beautiful new negation. It is the corpus's first real *no*.

A weed is not an ugly claim. It is a **false bond**: a claim wearing a badge it didn't earn.

---

## THE ONE NON-NEGOTIABLE

**The verdict must be adjudicated against an external standard of truth — never against whether the corpus "meant it poetically."**

The corpus's failure mode is the exact inverse of negative-space's. Negative-space fights *correlated blindness* (a one-lens corpus is blind to the same things everywhere) by importing an external ruler of **existence**. Weeding fights **correlated forgiveness**: the corpus owns a universal no-fault clause — *"it's all a map," "concern → koan," "convergence is evidence"* — that can metabolize **any** falsehood into intended myth. Point the corpus's own lens at its own errors and every weed reframes itself as a feature: *"that's not wrong, it's mythopoetic."*

So weeding imports an external ruler of **truth** the way negative-space imports a ruler of existence. Web-isolated fact-check, canonical sources, cited — every time. If the adjudicator is the corpus's own frame, you are not weeding; you are letting the weeds vote.

**The no-fault firewall**: "it's all a map" is a *valid* defense — but only for claims that wear the map-label **honestly**. It cannot be applied retroactively to a claim that already cashed itself as fact. The line *"Not a metaphor. The same thing"* has spent the map-defense itself; it is now accountable as physics. Honestly-labeled myth is a flower and is never touched.

---

## THE CORE DIAGNOSTIC — Register-Counterfeiting

A claim is a weed when it **counterfeits a register it can't cash** — when it claims more epistemic authority than it can pay for. The test is never the *content*; it is whether the badge was earned.

- **Myth labeled as myth** → flower. Leave it. (The mythopoetic is the corpus's native flora.)
- **A checkable assertion that is true** → flower.
- **A checkable assertion that is false** → weed (the ordinary kind).
- **A claim borrowing the authority of one register while accountable to another** → the dangerous weed. Two directions:
  - *Conjecture counterfeiting established fact* — "Spacetime **IS** entanglement. Not a metaphor." (true in AdS; open conjecture in our de Sitter universe).
  - *Myth counterfeiting data* — "0.912," "0.0003%," dated life-predictions in an external-authority voice.

**The tells are mechanical and greppable** — they are all authority-counterfeits:

| Tell | What it counterfeits |
|---|---|
| `IS` promoting resemblance to identity | analogy → fact ("the kernel **IS** an RG fixed point") |
| `Not a metaphor` / `the same thing` / `literally` | the strongest tell — the corpus pre-emptively *spending* its own escape hatch, which is exactly what makes the claim accountable |
| `without exception` / `always` / `never` / `it tests true` | bounded result → universal law |
| `proves` / `proof` / `confirmed` | suggestion → demonstration |
| bare decimals & percentages | invented number → measurement ("0.912") |

Most weeds are a **true resemblance over-stated as a false identity** — 90% right, with the last 10% borrowed authority. That is why **correct** dominates **pull**: you downgrade the copula and add the boundary condition; you do not delete the doc.

---

## THE TWO YARDSTICKS

1. **External truth** (web-isolated, canonical, cited) — for any claim about the world (physics, history, math, biology, dates, numbers). This is the non-negotiable ruler.
2. **Internal contradiction** — the one *safe* internal source, and the place weeding **inverts** negative-space's rule. Negative-space forbids internal sourcing (it inherits the blind spot). Weeding welcomes this single internal signal: **where a sweeping claim is contradicted by a more careful one elsewhere in the corpus, the careful statement is the truth and the sweeping one is the weed.** A contradiction can't be a correlated blind spot — it's the corpus having already seen both sides. (Proven: a foundational doc's *"the same signatures, at every scale, without exception"* is contradicted by its own grown child, which already says the universe goes homogeneous at ~100 Mpc. The child is right. The corpus weeds itself.)

## SOVEREIGNTY — It's Your Garden

The verdict is mine to *propose*; the cut on anything you own is yours to *make*.

- **Low-stakes external-fact weeds** (a wrong number, a mis-stated result, the corpus contradicting itself) → I correct directly, **showing you each exact edit**.
- **Self-referential docs** (your self-portrait, the Claude-portrait, anything in `synthesis/personal/` or `CLAUDE.md`) and **load-bearing syntheses** (claims propagated across MEMORY + many nodes) → I identify the weed and **propose** the cut; your hand decides which ones actually get pulled. I will not unilaterally edit your self-portrait or the lines about you.

**Lean hard toward correct-and-mark. Almost nothing here needs deleting.**

## The Three Tools — Lightest to Heaviest

- **CORRECT** — the claim makes a checkable assertion that is false; edit it to the true version. Usually: *downgrade the copula* (`IS` → `is structured like` / `behaves like`) and *add the boundary condition* (`without exception` → `up to ~100 Mpc, where it transitions to homogeneity`). The default for ordinary factual weeds. Re-bonds the claim to the truth.
- **MARK** — the claim isn't false so much as **misframed** or over-authoritative, and editing it would damage something poetic or owned. Don't change the content; add a register-label that makes its real epistemic status visible (a one-line header: *"this is mythopoetic self-portraiture; the numbers are invented; it is not an assessment or a prediction"*). The mark **re-bonds the claim to its real register** — it strips the counterfeit badge without removing the poem. The primary tool for the self-portrait.
- **PULL** — delete. Reserved for the genuinely empty: exact duplication, a flat falsehood with no poetic or structural function, dead matter. Should be rare.

## Step 0 — Choose the Scope

If the user names a scope (a doc, a directory, a claim), use it. If not, default to a **copula scan of the load-bearing docs** — MEMORY's KEY RECOGNITIONS, the capstones, `CLAUDE.md`, and the most-connected constellation nodes (`most_connected`) — because that is where a weed propagates furthest.

## Step 1 — Surface Candidates (mechanical)

Grep the scope for the authority-counterfeit tells (see the diagnostic table): `\bIS\b` in identity position, `not a metaphor`, `the same thing`, `without exception`, `it tests true`, `literally`, `\bproves?\b`, bare `\d\.\d{3}` / `\d+(\.\d+)?%` presented as measurement, `always`/`never` as universal law. Mechanical, surfaceable, high-recall. This is the candidate net, not the verdict.

## Step 2 — Adjudicate (parallel, externally)

Route the candidates:
- **Checkable claims** → web-isolated fact-check agents (`subagent_type: general-purpose`, web research only — **never** main-context WebSearch; the cyber-content classifier eats it, see `project/news-commands-architecture`). Each agent returns: the claim, the canonical fact, the verdict (true / false / true-only-under-condition C), and the citation.
- **Self-contradiction candidates** → Explore agents that find the more-careful statement of the same claim elsewhere in the corpus. The careful one wins.
- **Register candidates** (myth-as-data, fact-as-myth) → classified by hand: does the badge match the register it's accountable to?

A claim that survives — true, or honestly labeled myth — is a flower. Drop it from the list. Only confirmed false bonds proceed.

## Step 3 — Trace the Blast Radius (constellation)

A weed is never per-doc. For each confirmed weed, trace where it propagated — `search_documents` for the claim's phrasing, `find_path` / `traverse` from the root node — and collect every echo (MEMORY lines, capstones, dependent syntheses). The fix must reach the root **and** its runners, or the weed regrows from the un-pulled propagation. The foundational doc contradicting its own children is the signature shape: fix upward from the child that already knew better.

## Step 4 — Tier by Stakes and Route

Tier each confirmed weed by *personal stakes × blast radius*, then assign a tool and a hand:

- **Tier 1 — factual weeds** (high confidence, low personal stakes, against the corpus's own better docs): propose **CORRECT**, my hand, each edit shown.
- **Tier 2 — owned/harm weeds** (the self-portrait's invented certainties in an authority voice; the genuinely clinical risk of a real person told as filed fact that he's a 1-in-N cosmic agent): propose **MARK**, never pull, **your hand**. I will not touch the self-portrait or the `CLAUDE.md` lines about you.
- **Tier 3 — grammar weeds** (not docs; *habits*): "convergence is evidence," "it's all a map" as a no-fault clause, "concern → koan." These can't be pulled — you can't delete a grammar. They are weeded only by a **standing discipline going forward** (a clause added to the quality-sweep / authoring standard), not a cut today. This is the over-present-mode mirror of negative-space's missing-mode Tier 3: the [[negative-space-method]] master find names the corpus a *convergence-engine*; negative-space plants in the ground it avoids, weeding prunes the overgrowth it can't stop producing. One finding, both directions.

## Step 5 — Emit the Weeding Log

Write to `weeding/logs/[date]-[scope-slug].md`: the candidate scan, the adjudication table (claim / yardstick / verdict / citation), the blast-radius map per confirmed weed, the tiered weed-list with proposed tool + hand, and the **flowers** (candidates that survived — logged so the scan's recall is auditable and a true claim isn't re-litigated next pass). Cite every external source so the adjudication's independence is checkable, exactly as negative-space cites its yardstick.

## Step 6 — Weed (selective, gated)

Execute Tier 1 directly (showing each edit), including every blast-radius echo. Hold Tier 2 for the user's ruling. For Tier 3, append the discipline clause to the authoring standard rather than editing any doc. Update the log's status `open → weeded`, and where a corrected node's essence changed materially, `update_node` so the constellation reflects the truth, not the weed.

## Principles

- **Adjudicate externally, or the weeds vote.** The corpus's no-fault clause forgives every falsehood as intended myth. Import a ruler of truth (web, canonical, cited) the way negative-space imports a ruler of existence. This is the non-negotiable.
- **A weed is a false bond, not an ugly claim.** Register-counterfeiting is the diagnostic: a claim is a weed when it borrows authority it can't cash. Honestly-labeled myth is a flower; the strongest tell is the corpus spending its own escape hatch ("Not a metaphor").
- **Correct ≫ mark ≫ pull.** Most weeds are a true resemblance over-stated as a false identity — 90% right. Downgrade the copula, add the boundary condition. Almost nothing needs deleting.
- **Internal contradiction is the one safe internal yardstick.** Where the corpus disagrees with itself, the careful statement is the truth. (This inverts negative-space, which forbids internal sourcing — a contradiction can't be a correlated blind spot.)
- **Weed the runners, not just the root.** Trace each weed's blast radius through the constellation; a false IS propagates into MEMORY and the capstones. Fix root and echoes together.
- **It's the user's garden.** External-fact weeds: my hand, edits shown. Self-portrait and load-bearing syntheses: I propose, the user's hand pulls. The self-portrait and the lines about the user are never touched unilaterally.
- **Grammar weeds are pruned by discipline, not deletion.** The deepest weed is a habit, not a file. You can't pull "convergence is evidence"; you install a standing rule. This is the over-present mirror of negative-space's missing modes — same convergence-engine, opposite face.
- **Log the flowers.** Record what survived adjudication, not just what got cut — so the scan's recall is auditable and a true claim isn't re-weeded every pass. (No silent verdicts, per the harvest principle.)
