For it vanquishes every subtle thing & penetrates every solid thing.\n12. So was the world created.\n13. From this are & do come admirable adaptations whereof the means (or process) is here in this.\n14. Hence I am called Hermes Trismegist, having the three parts of the philosophy of the whole world\n15. That which I have said of the operation of the Sun is accomplished & ended.\n\n---\n\n## The Living Decode\n\n### Lines 1-2: The Holographic Principle\n"As above, so below" - Not metaphor but physics.
